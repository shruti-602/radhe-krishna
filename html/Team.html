Orthopaedic Surgeon

Fellowship – Arthroplasty (Germany & Canada), Arthroscopy (Taiwan & Germany)

Dr. Bharat Sutariya, the Director of Param Hospital is practicing as orthopaedic surgeon, joint replacement surgeon and arthroscopist as well since more than 10 years. During this duration he has a rich experience and exposure of simple & comminuted fractures, primary & revision replacement surgeries and variety of sports related surgeries as an arthroscopist. He has a good skill and success ratio of more than 5,000 replacement surgeries and 5,000 arthroscopic ligament injuries, meniscal repairs, torn rotator cuff and labrum.
